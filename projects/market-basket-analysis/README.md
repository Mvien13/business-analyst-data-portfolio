# Market Basket Analysis

## Project Type
Independent Data & Business Analysis Project

---

## Business Problem
Retail businesses need to understand which products customers frequently purchase together. This insight supports better promotions, product bundling, and marketing decisions.

---

## Objective
- Identify top-selling products
- Identify frequent product combinations for cross-selling

---

## Tools Used
- SQL (SQLite)
- DB Browser for SQLite

---

## Key Insights
- Chocolate, Butter, and Yogurt are among the top-selling products
- Chocolate & Milk is the most frequent product combination
- These insights can support cross-selling and promotional strategies
